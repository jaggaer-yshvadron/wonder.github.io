WARM UP - TEAM WONDER - We show up and show them who we are.

🔥

Mindset

  • 🗣 Talk early, talk loud.
  • ⚡ Move with purpose.
  • 🤝 Stay connected.
🏃‍♀️

1. Team Energy & Movement · 4 min

  • Line up on the baseline in one line.
  • 🏃‍♀️ Run to net then 🔙 backpedal, 2 times.
  • 🏃‍♀️ Run to net then ⚡ sprint back, 2 times.
  • Stay together. No one finishes alone.
  • 🦵 10 team squats, count loud.
  • 🤝 Pair up for plank hold or wall sit, 20 to 30 seconds together.
  • Finish with 📣 WONDER!
🏐

2. Ball Warm-Up · 4 min

  • With a partner, share 1 ball.
  • 🤲 Controlled passing with focus on platform and target.
  • 🔺 Aim to chest or setter target.
  • 🏀 Ball slams to the floor, 10 each, alternating turns.
🎯

3. Net Work · 5 min

  • In lines: 🎯 toss, 🏃‍♀️ approach, then 💥 controlled attack.
  • Progression: ✋ catch and form, then 💥 full approach and hit to space.
  • Other side: 🧱 serve receive into target pass.
  • Focus on 🗣 calling the ball and ➡️ moving forward with momentum.
🎯

4. Serve · 1-2 min

  • 🎯 Serve to zones and call your spot before serving.
  • 🧱 Teammates pass and control the ball.
🤝

5. Team Lock-In · 1 min

  • 🤝 Quick huddle.
  • 🧠 1 focus word.
  • 📣 Break together strong.
